**Explicit Preferences**
* Authentic 8-bit/chiptune sound.
* Music that evokes classic arcade or Nintendo gameplay (e.g., level backgrounds, boss fights, level select screens).
* Strong, memorable, and catchy melodies.
* Retro video game nostalgia.
* Themes of triumphant moments or iconic game soundtracks.
* Styles similar to 2D racing games or platformers.

**Negative Preferences**
* Modern electronic/dance tracks with chiptune elements layered on top.
* "Club" or high-energy house vibes.
* Overwhelmingly frenetic or high-pressure/fast-action intensity.

**Hard Constraints**
* Must sound like it could actually fit within an old arcade or Nintendo game rather than being a modern interpretation.
