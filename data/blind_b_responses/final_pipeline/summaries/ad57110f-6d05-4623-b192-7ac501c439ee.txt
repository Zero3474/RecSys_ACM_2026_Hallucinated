**Explicit Preferences**
* Rock music with a theatrical or dramatic feel.
* Vocals that are performed or narrated rather than just sung.
* Songs that tell a story.
* Music that builds and releases tension.
* Unexpected musical twists, shifts in instrumentation, or changes in pace.
* High intensity and impactful dynamics.

**Negative Preferences**
* Standard energetic singing without dramatic flair.
* Straightforward pop-punk tracks that lack theatricality or dynamic shifts.
