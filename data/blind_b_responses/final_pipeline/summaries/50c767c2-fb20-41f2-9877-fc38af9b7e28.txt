**Explicit Preferences**
*   Genre: Downtempo electronic, ambient, instrumental electronic.
*   Mood/Atmosphere: Moody, immersive, cinematic, introspective, melancholic, deep, and expansive.
*   Sonic Characteristics: Smooth, layered, intricate sound design, dense soundscapes, and deep atmospheric synths.
*   Rhythmic/Textural Elements: Subtle trip-hop influence, organic or glitchy textures, and "found sound" percussion.

**Negative Preferences**
*   Music that is "just ambient" without a distinctive sonic palette or depth.
*   Tracks that lack a sense of melancholy or introspection.
