**Explicit Preferences**
* Songs with captivating, impactful, and powerful lyrics.
* Lyrical themes: storytelling, deep emotions, introspection, self-discovery, overcoming struggles, finding inner strength, and romantic love.
* Open to different artists and different musical genres.

**Hard Constraints**
* Lyrics must be the primary focus of the recommendation.
